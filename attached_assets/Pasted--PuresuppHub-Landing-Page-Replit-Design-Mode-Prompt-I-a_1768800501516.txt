## Critical Design Instructions:

1. **Photography style is CRITICAL:** All product images must look like the reference - studio quality, soft natural lighting, clean backgrounds with subtle gradients
2. **Asymmetric layouts:** Don't make everything centered - use the 40/60 split pattern
3. **Generous spacing:** Sections feel "airy" with 120px+ vertical padding
4. **Italic emphasis:** Use `<em>` tags for emphasis words in headlines, NOT `<strong>`
5. **Natural color palette:** Stick to the earthy greens and warm neutrals - no bright primary colors except for badges
